In 1990, 14-year-old Leah awakens to find her mother weeping over the death of her father in a terrorist attack. In 1997, a covert agent posing as a French maid carries out a flawless assassination of a former KGB operative, but one man’s fleeting recognition ensures she can't return to her old identity. Sometimes the family you find becomes the bridge to redemption and home. Sometimes the past doesn't just knock on your door, it bursts through with guns firing. Once an elite assassin, now a woman running from her past, Ronnie discovers that coming home might be the one mission she can’t complete alone.
