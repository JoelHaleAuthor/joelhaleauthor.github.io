Spanning decades and generations, the Lost and Found series follows a family bound by love, loyalty, and a secret ability most people only experience by instinct—the power to stretch time in moments that matter most. For Ronnie Clay, already a gifted musician, mastering that control transforms her into a covert operative capable of reshaping fate itself. Alongside Alex Noone and later their daughters, she learns that power is never just a gift, it’s a responsibility that tests every bond they share. Blending espionage, humor, emotional depth, and the quiet strength of women who refuse to yield, the Lost and Found series is a sweeping thriller about family, choice, and the timeless fight to protect what you love.
